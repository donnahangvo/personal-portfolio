About Me

My name is Donna Vo. I am currently a student at Coding Temple doing a Self-Paced Course. My goal with Coding Temple is to build a solid foundation of understanding of how coding works and how to code so that I can find a position in the tech industry. My goal is to combine my coding knowledge with my extensive management experience to work in a field that I have always had an interest in. My extensive management background comes from almost 10 years in the construction industry performing many different roles that have allowed me to learn how to work with many different facets of a project team to build structures that are used by people on a constant basis.
